Who are we?

  • PMR Industries Pty Ltd (PMR) is an Australian owned and operated entity founded in 2020 manufacturing and distributing cable and its associated accessories.

What we do?

  • As your trusted partner in delivering quality cables and cable accessories, we ensure every customer is offered the best service, best price and a quality product.

What we value?

  • Health, Safety, Quality and the Environment
  • Our employees and their development.
  • Our clients' needs and expectations.
  • Teamwork and performance
  • Integrity and Respect
  • Accountability
  • Innovation and Leadership

Quality

Our highly accredited manufacturers deliver superior quality cables and products and hold ISO 9001, ISO 14001 and ISO IEC 17025 accreditations.

Experience

With a collective experience of more than 60 years working across Australian mining, telecom and industrial markets, our mission is to be a reliable and customer focussed company to service the Australian market. PMR endeavours to work with the customer to provide quality products that the customers can depend on.

WHS

Our greatest asset is our people and we continually strive for safety through leadership, transparency, continuous improvement and ZEROHARM.

Environmental management

We directly support the environment by integrating environmental responsibility into day-to-day decisions and long-term goals across all activities and disciplines especially sourcing from ethically and environmentally conscious suppliers
